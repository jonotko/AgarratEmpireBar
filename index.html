Party Bar

Throwing a party and need a bar?. We provide both alcoholic and non-alcoholic beverages for your needs. Our party bar consist of an actual front bar giving your customers a real bar experience. Unlike other caterers we don't use tables and cloth; instead we have a custom built bar top from which we serve.

Small Events Catering

Small events cover a broad range of functions. Maybe you're having an office party, a wedding, a birthday party, a family day, a christmas party, a house party...just add party to end of noun and you can call us.

Event Planning

Sometimes the planning process can be arduous We can help! Our team consist of certified event planners who can give you advice both from the books and experience. Give us a call we'll be happy to help.
